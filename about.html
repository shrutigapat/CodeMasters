About the Conference

Our story, mission, and what makes CodeMasters unlike any other tech event.

Our Mission

Built By Developers, For Developers

CodeMasters Conference was born from a simple idea: what if there was a conference where every session delivered real, actionable value? No fluff. No sales pitches. Just world-class engineering knowledge shared openly.

Since our inaugural event in 2019 with 400 attendees, we've grown into the world's most anticipated independent developer conference — drawing over 5,000 engineers, architects, and product builders from 60+ countries each year.

We are proudly community-driven and independently organized, ensuring our content serves you — the developer — above all else.

Our Journey

A Brief History

2019

The First CodeMasters

400 attendees, 20 speakers, 1 track. A small but mighty beginning in Austin, TX.

2021

Going Global & Virtual

Pivoted to a fully virtual format and reached 18,000 live viewers from 90 countries.

2023

Hybrid Format Launched

3,200 in-person + 12,000 virtual attendees. Introduced the Hackathon and Career Expo.

2026

CodeMasters 2026 — Biggest Yet

San Francisco. 5,000+ in person. 80+ speakers. 3 days. Our most ambitious event to date.
